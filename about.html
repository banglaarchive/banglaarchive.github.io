Purpose

In 2020, one day out of nowhere, I felt a need to preserve Bangla/Bengali contents, especially the great works in Bangla or Bengali literature. I believe if we don't digitalize these contents, then those works will disappear and vanish with time. I also believe these works should be available to the public at "zero" cost. Thus, the idea of The Bangla Archive came into being.

Vision

The vision for The Bangla Archive is to become the biggest provider for Bangla/Bengali books and articles to the public at zero cost.

Action Plan

The plan is to crawl the web for Bangla/Bengali contents available to download for free and make them available through this website. You are also requested to support by sending books and articles to The Bangla Archive. All files will be scanned for virus using VirusTotal to ensure files are clean.

Soon, in the future, we are going to self-host the contents, and integrate alternative technologies, such as BitTorrent, IPFS etc.
